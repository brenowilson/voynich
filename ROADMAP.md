# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation
- establish repository boundaries and provenance rules;
- catalog primary and scientific sources;
- define observational schemas and uncertainty vocabulary.

## Phase 1 — Primary-source acquisition
- obtain canonical high-resolution images from the holding institution;
- create checksums and a page/folio manifest;
- document transformations without altering source files.

## Phase 2 — Independent observation
- register pages and text-bearing regions;
- segment lines and candidate glyph instances;
- record ambiguity without forcing merges or splits;
- freeze pilot folios before external comparison.

## Phase 3 — Structural representations
- construct spatial, sequential, recurrence and graph representations;
- define operator families from explicit data-generating rules;
- quantify sensitivity to segmentation and classification choices.

## Phase 4 — Spectral and mathematical analysis
- compute spectra, modes, gaps, multiplicities and stability statistics;
- compare against null models and perturbations;
- search for reproducible invariants across pages, regions and scales.

## Phase 5 — Isolated external comparison
- align frozen observations with external transliterations and published claims;
- measure agreement, disagreement and representation dependence;
- preserve the independent pipeline unchanged.
