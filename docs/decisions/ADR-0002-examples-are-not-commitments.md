# ADR-0002: Examples are not formal commitments

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-03

## Context
Exploratory discussion uses familiar words and mathematical objects to approximate ideas that may be more general than available terminology.

## Decision
Interpret examples by their intended structural role. Do not convert an analogy into a definition, ontology, operator or claim without explicit formalization.

## Consequences
Discussion remains generative while the canonical model remains precise. Formal commitments must appear in schemas, definitions, protocols, hypothesis records or ADRs.
