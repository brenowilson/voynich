# Uncertainty Protocol

Use explicit states rather than silent guesses:

- `observed`: directly visible property;
- `derived`: deterministic from recorded observations;
- `provisional`: current choice may change;
- `ambiguous`: multiple supported alternatives;
- `unresolved`: insufficient evidence;
- `excluded`: omitted by a documented rule.

Probabilities or confidence scores require a calibration method. They are not substitutes for alternative annotations.
